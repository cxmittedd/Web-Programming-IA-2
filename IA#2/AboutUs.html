About us

The idea of Fresh cuts came about when after a month of calling our lawn care guy, the lawn was still uncut. I realized that unprofessional lawn care work was prevalent across Jamaica and a solution was needed.Fresh cuts, what was once just an idea to revolutionize lawn cutting is now Jamaica's leading lawn cutting service. With over 200 active employees, we ensure your Lawn is cut On time, EveryTime!

How does it all work?

After Registering and logging into your account, the 'services' option will appear where you can then schedule and pay for your lawn to be cut.Your task will then be sent to our database where an Agent from our team will fulfil your task according to the schedule you created.
